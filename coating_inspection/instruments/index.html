Elcometer™ Ultrasonic Thickness Gauges

Elcometer™ Ultrasonic Thickness Gauges

measures thickness of materials like metals, platics, glass, etc. using compatible transducer through the following modes:

  • Pulse Echo (PE) - standard method of measuring material
  • Interface Echo (IE) - displays the total thickness from the top
  • Echo to Echo (EE) - ignores the thickness of coated materials
  • PLAS Mode - mode specifically designed for very thin plastics
