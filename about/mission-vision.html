Mission

A dynamic, creative and responsive Catholic educational institution where leader, workers, students and parents thrive in communal synergy to achieve total human development.

Vision

To promote total human development through quality and Christ-centered education

Goals

  • To provide a Christian formation program for students, faculty, staff and parents
  • To provide opportunities for professional growth and personal enhancement for faculty and staff
  • To adopt and utilize current and progressive technological trends in education
  • To build and acquire adequate school facilities and equipment
  • To establish programs that will promote sports, the performing arts and other interests
  • To secure accreditation from PAASCU and other prestigious accrediting bodies
  • To foster awareness of and pride in Filipino nationalism
  • To engage in partnerships with the larger community in addressing environmental and other socio-civic concerns
  • To affiliate and actively participate in the programs of relevant educational associations (e.g. CSAAP, EVAPS, CEAP, etc.)
  • To provide scholarships/grants to deserving students
  • To forge a strong and effective partnership between the school and the home
